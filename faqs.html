Frequently Asked Questions



1: What do I need to open a Ribbons account?

To open a Ribbons account, you need to tell us your Name, Email,Your Phone number, Create a password, and tell us if you are the groom or bride.

2: How much does Ribbons cost?

The website, the dashboard and tools on the platform are completely free.

3: What's the catch? How do you make money?

So we provide you with a gift wishlist store(registry) on the website, where friends and family can buy you gifts online from anywhere on the world. We charge 10% the value of those gifts when you convert them to cash.

4: Do you convert the gifts bought to cash?

Yes, you can convert all the gifts bought for you to cash, almost all our couples have jumped on that option.

5: How secure is this app?

We have a SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) certificate on our website to secure private communication between you and our servers. We also working with a certified payment processing partner; PayStack which secures all payments that passes through our platform.

6: How long can I keep the website?

Yes, you can convert all the gifts bought for you to cash, almost all our couples have jumped on that option.

7: How do I get immediate help when I have a question?

We are here for you, so please reach us on WhatsApp or Call us: +2349099020813 ,
Email: holla@withribbons.com

8: What happens to the gifts bought on the platform?

Immediately gifts are bought on the couples website, it reflects on the couples dashboard, they get an email notification of the gift bought and who bought the gift and the guest that bought the gifts also gets a Thank You message. After the wedding ceremony, the couple can request for the gifts to be delivered to their specified address or they request for the cash equivalent of the gift.

9: How do I monitor the gifts bought for?

Once you login, you get access to your dashboard, from this dashboard you can monitor all the activities going on on your wedding website; Who has RSVP, what well wish messages were sent and by who, what gifts were bought and by who and more.

10: Can I collaborate with my husband and event planner?

We are working on building and including this feature and more on the platform to make it an amazing experience for you to seamlessly collaborate with everyone.
